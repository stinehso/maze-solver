# maze-solver
Solves mazes from input files, with GUI.<br>
Click anywhere on the white spaces, and it will color the shortest way out.

### example input file
8 9<br>
#####. ###<br>
#.  . .  . . # . #<br>
#. #####. #<br>
#. # . . . . . #<br>
#. #. ###. #<br>
#. #. #. #. #<br>
#. . . . . # . #<br>
#########<br>
